Save work plan information

Work plan draft Employer selection, learner role pathway, dates, documents, and job details are stored as part of the work plan record. Draft work plans remain editable and visible to CPA Staff but do not enter the review queue until submitted.
Submission workflow After submission, the work plan becomes read-only, status updates to Pending Mentor Approval, mentor review workflow begins, and reminder notifications are scheduled for 30, 60, and 90 days if no action is taken. Learners have 3 resubmission attempts.
